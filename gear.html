Everyday Carry

These are the tools I use daily or rotate through depending on where I’m going. Nothing here is aspirational. Everything earns its place through use.

Tech

• Phone – iPhone SE (3rd Gen)
• Secondary Phone – iPhone 5 (offline media, experiments)
• Laptop – ThinkPad (Linux-first workflow)
• Wired Earbuds / Headphones
• USB-C flash drive (local files, backups)
• Charging cable (one, not five)

Stationery

• Pocket notebook (unlined or grid)
• Mechanical pencil
• Pen that I don’t worry about losing
• Folded paper (notes, ideas, lists)

Cameras

• 35mm film camera
• Compact digital camera
• One prime lens I know well
• Extra roll of film or spare SD card

Bag Carry

• Backpack or messenger (no excess pockets)
• Headphones case
• Notebook folder
• Camera when I expect to walk

Miscellaneous

• Keys (nothing decorative)
• Wallet (cards + folded cash)
• Bandana or cloth
• Bike lock
